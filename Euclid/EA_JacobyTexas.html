Jacoby/Texas Transfers

  1. Defined

    1. JT!! is a Jacoby transfer bid of 2♦!! or 2♥!!
    2. TT!! is a Texas transfer bid of 4♦!! or 4♥!!

  2. Weak Hands

    1. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - pass has 0-7 HCP with 5+ of M

  3. Invitational Hands

    1. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - 2NT has 8-9 HCP with 5 of M
    2. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - 3M has 8-9 HCP with 6 of M

  4. Game Bids, no slam interest

    1. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - 3NT shows 10+ HCP, no slam interest and only 5 cards in the major. Partner passes or bids 4M.
    2. 1NT - TT!!, 4M - pass shows 6+ M, 10+ HCP OR 7+ M with less.
    3. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - new suit (no jump). This new suit is forcing and shows a hand with all values in this suit and the transferred suit. Tells partner to pick 4M over 3NT if he cannot deal with the other 2 suits.

  5. Slam Tries

    1. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - 4M (mild slam try)
      • Opener should go on only if they are max AND have 3 trump AND have a doubleton.
    2. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - 4NT is quantitative (balanced slam try).
      • You have a hand with no singleton or void and balanced strength to go with your 5+ card major.
      • Partner can chooose between pass (minimum hand, 2 of your major), 5M (minimum hand, 3 of your major), 6M (max hand, 3 of your major) and 6NT (max hand, 2 of your major).
    3. 1NT - JT!!, 2M - jump in a suit! (unbalanced slam try)
      • If the transfer was to hearts, then these jumps are 3♠!, 4♣! and 4♦!
      • If the transfer was to spades, then they are 4♣!, 4♦! and 4♥!
      • The jump shows a singleton or void in the bid suit along with slam interest.
      • Should have 6 card major.
      • Opener should cue cheapest ace if he has one.
    4. 1NT - TT!!, 4M - 4NT is RKC Blackwood (strong slam try).
      • You have a hand where all you care about is partner's key cards.

  6. Superaccepts

    1. In accepting a Jacoby transfer, the following bids are used:
      1. 2M (normal accept) = 3 or fewer trumps
      2. 3M! = 4 trumps with a minimum (15 to 16-)
        • 1NT - 2♦!!, 3♥! shows 4 hearts, but minimum values
      3. 2NT! = 4 trumps, maximum values, 4-3-3-3 shape
        • 1NT - 2♦!!, 2NT! shows 4 hearts, a flat hand, and maximum values
      4. any other suit! shows 4 trumps, maximum values, and shortness (2 cards) in the bid suit
        • 1NT - 2♦!!, 2♠! shows 4 hearts, 2 spades, and maximum values
        • 1NT - 2♦!!, 3♦! shows 4 hearts, 2 diamonds, and maximum values
