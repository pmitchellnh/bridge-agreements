Defense Over (Typically Strong) 1NT Openers - D.O.N.T.

  1. Definitions

    1. DONT Overcall Strength
      1. 6+ non-vulnerable (11+ is considered a maximum)
      2. 8+ vulnerable (13+ is considered a maximum)
      3. Should be stronger if 4/5 or 5/4
      4. Could be weaker if 6/5 or 5/6 or 6/6
      5. If 4/5 or 5/4 do not use DONT if vulnerable AND minimum.
    2. Strength Needed by Advancer (Partner of DONT overcaller) for Game Tries.
      1. 14+ non-vulnerable
      2. 12+ vulnerable
      3. Note that ALL game tries start with a 2NT! bid and that all raises are preemptive

  2. Double! = Single-Suiter

    1. 6 or more cards in the suit
    2. Similar to but weaker than Cappelletti 2♣ in that the suit need not be solid or even semi-solid.
    3. Responses without further interfence
      1. Pass! for penalties with a strong hand 15 HCP or more
      2. 2♣! relay to find out what it is.
      3. 2♦!, 2♥!, 2♠! are natural and a strong 6-carder
    4. Response with additional interference
      1. Over an interfering redouble
        1. Pass! = bid your suit
        2. Suit bid = to play
      2. Over an interfering suit
        1. Double! = bid your suit
        2. Pass = does not wish to compete

  3. 2♣! = Clubs and a Higher Suit

    1. Responses without further interference
      1. Pass = willingness to play clubs
      2. 2♦! = bid your second suit
      3. 2♥, 2♠ are natural and to play
      4. 3♣ or even 4♣ are preemptive raises. They are NOT invitational.
      5. 2NT! = artificial asking bid game try
        1. 3♣! = minimum
        2. 3♦!, 3♥!, 3♠! = maximum and shows second suit.
    2. Responses with additional interference
      1. Over an interfering double
        1. Redouble! = bid your second suit
        2. Suit bid = to play
        3. Pass = willing to sit for clubs
      2. Over an interfering suit
        1. Double! = bid your second suit
        2. Pass = does not wish to compete

  4. 2♦! = Diamonds and a Major

    1. Responses without further interference
      1. Pass = willingness to play diamonds
      2. 2♥! = pass if second suit hearts, else bid 2♠ if second suit is spades
      3. 2♠ is natural and to play
      4. 3♦! or even 4♦! are preemptive raises. They are NOT invitational.
      5. 2NT! = artificial asking bid game try
        1. 3♣! = heart/diamond minimum
        2. 3♦! = spade/diamond minimum
        3. 3♥! = heart/diamond maximum
        4. 3♠! = spade/diamond maximum
    2. Responses with additional interference
      1. Over an interfering double
        1. Redouble! = bid your second suit
        2. Suit bid = to play
        3. Pass = willing to sit for diamonds
      2. Over an interfering suit
        1. Double! = Bid your second suit
        2. Pass = does not wish to compete

  5. 2♥! = Both Majors

    1. Responses without further interference
      1. Pass = prefers hearts
      2. 2♠ = prefers spades
      3. 3♣, 3♦ are natural and to play
      4. 3♥! or 3♠! are preemptive raises. They are NOT invitational.
      5. 2NT! = artificial asking bid game try
        1. 3♣! = minimum, hearts longer or better
        2. 3♦! = minimum, spades longer or better
        3. 3♥! = maximum, hearts longer or better
        4. 3♠! = maximum, spades longer or better
    2. Responses with additional interference
      1. Over an interfering double
        1. Redouble! = asks for a MINOR! Should be at least 5/6 or 6/5 in minors to make this bid. Note our second suit (spades) is known so that could have been bid if desired.
        2. Any other = to play
      2. Over an interfering suit
        1. Double! = To play. Note our 2nd suit is known
        2. Pass = does not wish to compete

  6. 2♠! = Single Spade Suit

    1. 6+ cards in spades with a minimum hand or terrible spades.
    2. With better hands, you'd start with * instead.
