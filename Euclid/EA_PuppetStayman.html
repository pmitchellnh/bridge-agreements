Puppet Stayman

  1. Defined:

    1. Over a 2NT opener, 3♣! is Puppet Stayman
    2. Puppet Stayman allows opener to open 2NT with a 5-card major
    3. This can allow partnership to find game across from a 5 or 6 point hand that might have passed a 1M opener (especially in 3rd or 4th seat)

  2. Responses:

    1. 3♦! = opener has at least one 4 card major, no 5-card major
      1. 3♥! = responder has spades.
        1. Opener can now bid 4♠ or 3NT to set game
        2. 3♠! = slam try (inviting cue bids)
        3. 4NT = RKC 1430.
      2. 3♠! = responder has hearts.
        1. Opener can now bid 4♥ or 3NT to set game
        2. 4♣ or 4♦ = first round control as a slam try (inviting cue bids)
        3. 4NT = RKC1430.
      3. 3NT to play
      4. 4♣! = responder is 4-4 in majors. Slam interest
      5. 4♦! = responder is 4-4 in majors. No slam interest. Bid 4M.
    2. 3♥! = opener has 5 hearts
      1. 3♠! slam invitational in hearts. Invites cues
      2. 3NT = to play
      3. 4♥ = to play
      4. 4NT = RKC 1430
    3. 3♠! = opener has 5 spades
      1. 3NT = to play
      2. 4♣, 4♦, 4♥ = slam invitation in spades. Shows first round control and invites cues.
      3. 4♠ = to play
      4. 4NT = RKC 1430
    4. 3NT! = opener denies a 4 or 5 card major
